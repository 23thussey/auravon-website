History

Auravon, founded on October 10, 2024, was originally named Tablosland. Over time, it grew into a structured micronation emphasizing innovation and governance by its Council.

Government Structure

The nation is led by the Primarch, supported by the Council of Innovators. Auravon's class system is divided into three main tiers:

  • Higher Class: The Primarch and the Council of Innovators.
  • Middle Class: The citizens of Auravon.
  • Lower Class: Criminals who serve in penal roles.

Economy and Currency

The national currency of Auravon is the Orlitz (⚛️). We welcome trade inquiries and partnerships.
